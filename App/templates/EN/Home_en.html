CALC - DIFF

Calc-Diff is a web application designed to calculate the diffusion coefficient and associated errors for chemical systems using the UNIFAC method. This method allows for the estimation of thermodynamic properties of substance mixtures based on activity models. The application provides an intuitive interface, helping users to perform these complex calculations easily, while delivering accurate results for professionals in chemistry and process engineering.
